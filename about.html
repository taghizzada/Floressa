About Floressa

For decades, Floressa has been your trusted supplier of flowers, plants, and accessories from top international manufacturers. As one of the largest wholesale suppliers in Europe, we offer an extensive assortment of cut flowers, potted plants, packaging materials, and floral accessories. Here, you’ll find everything you need for your floral business! We are committed to offering the highest quality products and excellent customer service to our retail and wholesale customers.
